FAQ

Questions, answered straight.

Everything you might want to know before booking a call — what Steady is, what it costs, how it works, and whether it's right for you. No fine print, no pitch.

What exactly is Steady?

Steady is 1:1 online nutrition coaching for busy professionals. It's not a diet or an app — it's a real coaching relationship that fixes the root causes of low energy, bloating, and inflammation using whole food, built around your actual schedule.

Is this a diet?

No. No foods are permanently off-limits and there's no plan to fall off of. We use real, whole food and change how you eat, not how much you're allowed to.

Do I have to count calories or track macros?

No — no macros spreadsheets and no daily logging. We focus on the few changes that actually move the needle so the right choices become automatic instead of a chore.

How much does coaching cost?

1:1 Coaching is $199/month on a 3-month program. The Pantry Reset is a one-time $150 session. Corporate Wellness is scoped and priced per team. The discovery call is always free. See the full breakdown →

Is coaching online? Where are you based?

Yes — coaching is 100% online over video, so we can work together wherever you are. I'm based in Central Time (CT).

How does the coaching actually work?

It starts with a free call, then a 90-minute deep-dive assessment, a custom protocol built around your schedule, biweekly video calls, and unlimited async support between sessions over voice and text. See The Steady Method →

How long until I see results?

Everyone's different, but most people who do the work notice steadier energy, fewer cravings, and less bloating within the first 2 to 4 weeks. The full three months is about making it last.

I travel and eat out constantly. Will this work?

That's exactly who it's built for. You get restaurant, travel, and airport playbooks tailored to you, so being on the road stops quietly derailing you.

Do I need blood work or labs?

No — labs aren't required. If you already have recent bloodwork, bring it and we'll use it. If not, we work from your symptoms, history, and daily patterns.

Can you help if I have diabetes or prediabetes?

Steady provides nutrition education and coaching, not medical care. If you have a diagnosed condition or take medication, we coordinate with — never replace — your doctor, since food changes can affect your treatment. Read Blood Sugar 101 for the basics.

What's your cancellation and refund policy?

The discovery call is free and cancellable anytime. 1:1 Coaching is a 3-month commitment billed monthly; monthly payments are non-refundable, and you can stop future billing with 7 days' notice after the initial term. The Pantry Reset can be rescheduled with 24 hours' notice. Full terms →

Book a free 20-minute discovery call. We'll talk through what's going on and whether Steady is the right fit — no pressure, no pitch.
