What Steady is

A coach, not an app.

Steady is 1:1 nutrition coaching for high-functioning professionals in their 30s to 50s who feel worse than they should. You work out sometimes. You don't eat fast food every day. But you're crashing after lunch, puffy in the morning, and bloated by 3pm — and you've stopped connecting it to food. We diagnose which patterns are driving your symptoms, then build a protocol that fits your travel, your client dinners, and your empty-by-Wednesday fridge.
