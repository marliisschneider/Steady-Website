July 5, 2026

The one change I'd make first

If you only did one thing, it would be this: eat 30 grams of protein at breakfast. Not oatmeal and fruit — eggs, Greek yogurt, or last night's leftovers. It flattens your blood sugar for the whole day, and most people are genuinely surprised how much steadier they feel by that first afternoon. It's the smallest change with the biggest return.

Why I put dairy back in

I cut dairy for years, convinced it was behind my inflammation. It wasn't. When I rebuilt my diet around whole foods and cut seed oils instead, I added full-fat dairy right back — and everything got better, not worse. It's a good reminder that the traditional foods are rarely the problem; the industrial stuff we added in the last seventy years usually is.
