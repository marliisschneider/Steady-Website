# Steady — Nutrition Coaching Website

## What is Steady

Steady is a nutrition coaching service for busy professionals who want to eat better without obsessing over food. It's not a diet plan or a calorie counter — it's a 1:1 coaching relationship that builds sustainable habits around real life: travel schedules, client dinners, irregular hours.

## Who It's For

Professionals in their 30s–40s who are high-functioning at work but running on caffeine and convenience food. They've tried diets, know what they should eat, but can't make it stick. They want a coach, not an app.

## What Makes It Different

No macros spreadsheets. No weekly weigh-ins. Steady works around the client's actual schedule and food preferences — the goal is consistency, not perfection.

## Services

- **1:1 Coaching** — 3-month engagement, biweekly calls + async check-ins via voice message
- **Pantry Reset** — single 90-min session to overhaul what's in the house
- **Corporate Wellness** — team programs for companies; custom pricing

## Tone

Calm, direct, warm. Not preachy, not hype-y. Like a knowledgeable friend who happens to know a lot about food.

## Website Goal

3-page static site (HTML/CSS/JS). Convince a busy professional to book a free 20-minute discovery call.

## Pages

- **index.html** — Hero + what Steady is + one clear CTA ("Book a free call")
- **about.html** — Coach background, philosophy, why this approach works
- **contact.html** — Simple contact form + Calendly embed placeholder for discovery call

## Design Direction

Clean, minimal. Navy or deep green tones. Lots of white space. No stock photo clichés (no salads, no measuring tape). Confident typography.

## Tech Stack

Plain HTML, CSS, and vanilla JS. No frameworks. One stylesheet (style.css). Mobile-responsive.
