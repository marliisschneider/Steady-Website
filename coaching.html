What to expect

What changes — and roughly when.

Every body and starting point is different, so treat this as the typical arc, not a promise. But for people who actually do the work, this is the shape of it.

  1. Week 1

    The first swaps

    We change your cooking oils, make one seed-oil swap, and get real protein into your breakfast. You won't feel transformed yet — but you've stopped pouring fuel on the fire, and the plan already fits your week.

  2. Weeks 2–4

    Energy steadies

    This is usually where it clicks. The 3pm crash softens, the constant cravings quiet down, and the morning puffiness starts to drop. It's the same window where my own joint pain and bloating disappeared years ago.

  3. Month 2

    It gets automatic

    The choices stop taking willpower. Eating out, traveling, and the empty-by-Wednesday fridge no longer derail you, because you've got playbooks for each. You're thinking about food a lot less.

  4. Month 3

    The new normal

    Steadier energy, less bloating, better sleep — and, most importantly, you understand why. That's what makes it last long after our three months are done.

Straight answers

Questions people ask before booking.

Is this a diet?

No. There's no diet to follow, no foods that are permanently off-limits, and no plan to fall off of. We fix the root causes of how you feel using real, whole food — built around your actual life.

Do I have to count calories or log every meal?

No macros spreadsheets and no daily logging. We focus on the handful of changes that actually move the needle, so the right choices become your default instead of a chore.

I travel constantly and eat out a lot. Will this actually work?

That's exactly who this is built for. You get restaurant, travel, and airport playbooks tailored to you, so being on the road and eating out stop quietly derailing you.

I've tried coaches and programs before and quit. Why is this different?

Most programs hand you a rigid plan and disappear. Steady adapts week to week around your real schedule, explains the why behind every change, and includes async support — so you're never stuck figuring it out alone.

Not the rigid, eat-this-exact-thing kind you'll resent by week two. You get frameworks, swaps, and go-to defaults that fit your taste and schedule — flexible enough to keep for good.
