# Steady — Strategy

## What Steady does + who it's for

Steady is 1:1 nutrition coaching for busy professionals in their 30s–50s who feel worse than they should — bloated by 3pm, crashing after lunch, foggy, inflamed — and who've tried diet apps and generic meal plans that assume a life they don't have.

We work around their actual schedule (travel, client dinners, empty fridges) to build sustainable habits around **inflammation, blood sugar dysregulation, and gut disruption** — the three root causes most people never address.

## Business model

Service-based coaching business. Three engagement tiers:

- **1:1 Coaching** — 3-month program, biweekly video calls + async voice check-ins
- **Pantry Reset** — single 90-min session to overhaul what's in the house
- **Corporate Wellness** — team programs for companies; custom pricing

Revenue is per-client, sold via 20-min free discovery calls. No paid ads yet — leaning on organic content + word of mouth.

## The ONE action I want a visitor to take

**Book a free 20-min discovery call.** Every page ends with this CTA. Secondary action: take the symptom quiz → routes them to the relevant Learn page → book call.

Long-term secondary: get on the email list → weekly value-driven notes → convert to call.

## Proof / story — what makes ME uniquely right to build this

I've lived in the trenches of trying to eat better while running a demanding career. I know the difference between advice that assumes you meal-prep on Sunday and advice that works for a professional who lands at 11pm.

The differentiator: **no macros spreadsheets, no weekly weigh-ins, no shame.** The goal is for clients to understand their body well enough that they don't need me forever. That's rare in a space where retention often depends on dependency.

I combine deep, research-backed education (see the Learn pages) with 1:1 coaching that meets clients where they actually are.

## Key differentiators (for the website)

1. **Root-cause focused** — inflammation / blood sugar / gut, not calories
2. **Educational-first** — the Learn pages teach the science upfront, not gated
3. **Real-life sustainable** — designed for travel, client dinners, chaotic schedules
4. **Human coach, not app** — 1:1 relationship, async voice for busy weeks
