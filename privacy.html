Privacy Policy

Plainly: we only collect what we need to reply to you and run the coaching, we never sell your data, and you can ask us to delete it at any time.

Last updated: July 3, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Steady Nutrition Coaching ("Steady," "we," "us") handles information when you visit this website or reach out to us. By using the site, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect

Information you give us. When you fill out a form or contact us, we collect the details you choose to provide — typically your name, email address, and any message you write.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we may collect basic technical data such as your browser type, device, approximate location, referring page, and how you move through the site. This helps us understand what's useful and fix what isn't.

2. How we use your information

  • To respond to your message and, if you ask, to arrange a discovery call.
  • To deliver and improve the coaching services you request.
  • To send information you specifically requested, such as a free guide or resource.
  • To understand site usage and improve our content.
  • To meet legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

3. How we share it

We do not sell your personal information. We share it only with trusted service providers who help us operate — for example, our website host, form and database provider, scheduling tool, and email software — and only to the extent they need it to do their job. We may also disclose information if required by law.

4. Cookies & analytics

The site may use cookies or similar technologies, including privacy-friendly analytics, to remember preferences and measure traffic. You can control cookies through your browser settings; blocking them may affect some functionality.

5. How long we keep it

We keep your information only as long as needed for the purposes above — to answer you, to provide coaching, and to meet legal requirements — and then delete or anonymize it.

6. Your choices & rights

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you, and to stop contacting you, at any time. Depending on where you live (for example, in California or the EU/UK), you may have additional rights over your data. To make a request, just email us using the contact below.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, but no method of transmission or storage is ever completely secure. We can't guarantee absolute security, and you share information with us at your own discretion.

8. Children

This site and our services are intended for adults. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we'll revise the "last updated" date above. Continued use of the site means you accept the current version.

10. Contact

Questions about your privacy, or want your data removed? Email hello@steadynutrition.co and we'll take care of it.

This policy is provided as general information and is not legal advice.
